About FACETRA

FACETRA is a premium gemstone trading house serving collectors, jewelers and investors seeking ethically sourced and internationally certified natural gemstones.

At FACETRA, gemstones are treated as rare natural assets shaped by geology, scarcity and global market demand — not mass retail products.

Our approach emphasizes authenticity, traceability and long-term value preservation.

We prioritize curated acquisition and verified documentation rather than volume-driven retail.
