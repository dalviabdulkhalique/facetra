About FACETRA

FACETRA is a premium gemstone trading house based in Mumbai, specializing in ethically sourced and internationally certified natural gemstones curated for collectors, jewelers and long-term investors.

Our Philosophy

At FACETRA, gemstones are regarded as natural assets shaped by time, geology and rarity. Every stone undergoes meticulous evaluation for authenticity, certification alignment, provenance and long-term appreciation potential.

What We Specialize In

  • Natural Diamonds with International Certification
  • Fine Rubies, Sapphires and Emeralds
  • Investment-grade Precious Gemstones
  • Rare Untreated and Collector Stones
  • Portfolio-grade Natural Assets

Ethics & Certification

FACETRA operates with strict transparency. All gemstones are responsibly sourced and aligned with recognized global laboratories such as GIA and IGI, ensuring confidence, traceability and compliance.

Leadership & Expertise

FACETRA is led by Abdul Khalique, Founder & Gemstone Consultant, combining generational gemstone knowledge with modern global trade practices to deliver verified natural assets of lasting value.

Partner With FACETRA

Whether sourcing fine jewelry or building gemstone portfolios, FACETRA provides professional consultation and verified assets.
