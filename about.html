About FACETRA

FACETRA is a premium gemstone trading house based in Mumbai, India, specializing in ethically sourced, internationally certified natural gemstones. We serve collectors, investors, and connoisseurs seeking authenticity, rarity, and long-term value.

Our Philosophy

At FACETRA, gemstones are regarded as timeless natural assets shaped by geological forces, rarity, and precision craftsmanship. Every stone undergoes meticulous evaluation for authenticity, certification alignment, provenance, and long-term appreciation potential.

What We Specialize In

  • Natural Diamonds with International Certification
  • Fine Rubies, Sapphires, and Emeralds
  • Investment-grade Precious Gemstones
  • Carefully Selected Semi-Precious Stones
  • Collector and Portfolio-grade Natural Assets

Ethics & Certification

Transparency and integrity are the foundation of FACETRA. Every gemstone offered is natural, responsibly sourced, and aligned with globally recognized certification standards. Our practices reflect strict compliance with ethical trade principles.

Leadership

FACETRA is led by Abdul Khalique, Founder & Gemstone Consultant, combining generational industry knowledge with modern global gemstone trade expertise.

Our Commitment

FACETRA exists to protect trust in gemstone trading. We do not chase volume — we curate value. Every gemstone represents legacy, discipline, and enduring worth.
