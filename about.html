About FACETRA

FACETRA is a premium gemstone trading house based in Mumbai, India, specializing in ethically sourced, internationally certified natural gemstones curated for discerning collectors.

Our Philosophy

At FACETRA, gemstones are regarded as natural assets shaped by time, geology, and rarity. Every stone is evaluated for authenticity, certification alignment, and long-term value.

What We Specialize In

  • Diamonds
  • Rubies
  • Sapphires
  • Emeralds
  • Selected semi-precious gemstones

Ethics & Certification

FACETRA operates with strict transparency. All gemstones are natural, responsibly sourced, and aligned with international certification standards.

Leadership

FACETRA is led by Abdul Khalique, Founder & Gemstone Consultant, combining traditional expertise with modern global trade practices.
